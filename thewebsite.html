What is Roblox Rap Battles?

What is Roblox Rap Battles you ask? Well it's where all the best soundcloud rappers come to get discovered, and if they are not at that point of their career yet, it is where they learn to freestyle like absolute Gods.

The Struggle of a Roblox Rapper

Chat Censor

Roblox rappers have to go through many struggles, the first, and possibly the biggest hurdle of theirs is the chat censor. Roblox rappers have been training themselves on how to get around this censor for years and once they think they have finally mastered it a curve ball is thrown at them. This clever censor hasn't been sleeping, it updates itself! Many rappers that didn't grow their career on Roblox use bad words such as the S word, the F bomb, and worst of all... potty language. This all has been blocked on Roblox, so the rappers coming out from that area are the masters of clean raps able for 5 year olds to responsibly hear.

Noob Judges

Often times a struggle for even the best of Roblox Rap Battler's is having noob judges, because they do not know how to properly rate the performances. This is a huge issue becuase at the highest level of Roblox rappers every performance counts, and one slip up can ruin someones ranking.
